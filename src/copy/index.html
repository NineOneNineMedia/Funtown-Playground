Meet The Owner Kate

My love for children started when my Niece was born in 2010. I cared for her as a loving Aunt and couldn’t wait for one day to give her a Cousin to play with. In 2014, I began caring for children as an AuPair. During this time, I learned how to keep children actively engaged with age appropriate activities. Over the next few years, I became a Step-Mother to our sons and a Mother to my energetic daughter. I realized it’s not always easy to keep them entertained with a smile on their faces. By putting these play sets together, I am providing fun and developing activities while adults can take a break, socialize and monitor their little ones.

My goal is to make sure that our clients are happy and your children are excited. Let me help make your next event less stressful and your children overwhelmed with joy.

Safety First

Our equipment is baby and kid-friendly. We set up on soft foam tiles and provide a colorful fence with gated access. All Soft Play elements are made from soft foam and covered by a strong layer of vinyl. This keeps children safe when they play. Internal elements are made from a wooden frame and hard plastics making them strong and durable.

Cleanliness

Clean and germ-free equipment is our top priority to help keep your children safe and healthy. We have a strict cleaning and disinfecting routine after each rental. All equipment is again sanitized  after set up.
