Welcome to Playhouse Rentals

I have a genuine love for children. It started when I became an aunt to my amazing niece, I cared for her like she was my own. In 2014, I began caring for children as an AuPair. During this time, I learned how to keep children actively engaged with age appropriate activities. When I became a mother to an energetic daughter and a step-mom to two boys, reality kicked in. I realized it’s not always easy to keep them entertained with a smile on their faces. By putting these play sets together, I am providing fun and developing activities while adults can take a break, socialize and monitor their little ones.

What We Offer

Funtown Playground offers the best kids party rental service in town! Our soft play equipment provides a fun and safe environment for crawlers, toddlers and children under the age of 6. It encourages little ones to play, feed their imagination and stimulate gross motor and social skills.

Safety First

Our equipment is baby and kid-friendly. We set up on soft foam tiles and provide colorful fence with a gate (optional).

Cleanliness

We have a strict cleaning and disinfecting routine after each rental. All equipment is again sanitized  after set up.
